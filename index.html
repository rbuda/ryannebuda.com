Selling Insurance to Engineering

I was good at sales - NOT great. I wasn't great as I had little passion for what I did. Insurance, or Employee Benefits as I would say in my elevator speech, became boring once life made me live and breath it. It took a year of faking it, a manager doing everything she could to get me to leave, and building my own computer for gaming to learn my passion lied elsewhere. Being honest with yourself is something one should learn to do. It took me until my 20's were about over for me to accept I just wasn't going to be great at this suit-and-tie-shaking-hands job.
